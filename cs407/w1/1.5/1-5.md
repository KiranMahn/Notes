# 1.5 Substitution Ciphers

## Caesar Cipher 
For this cipher letters of the alphabet are shifted by a key n (number of letters to shift by) 

example: 

> Input: abc
> Key: 3
> Output: def

## Vigenere Cipher 
Uses the ceasar cipher and maps a plaintext word to the keyword
